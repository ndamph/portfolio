Hi, there!

I'm Nadine, an accountant-turned front-end developer based in Winnipeg, Manitoba

I always knew that when I started my professional life in accounting, it wasn't the only profession I wanted to explore. Although I enjoy the challenges that accounting can bring, I found myself wanting to have more of a creative aspect to my job.

When I was thinking of what I wanted to pursue, I flashed back to middle-school computer class learning HTML, and the days of customizing piczo and tumblr sites. I remembered it to scratch that creativity-itch, while also being a fun challenge. I've then decided to take front-end workshops through SheCodes, which has since solidified my decision of pursuing front-end development professionally.

Although I am still working in accounting full-time, I would absolutely love taking on projects on the side!

While I'm not crunching numbers, or writing code, I enjoy knitting socks and watching reruns of my favourite shows!
